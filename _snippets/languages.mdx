## Supported languages

This model works in the following languages:

- English `en`
- Spanish `es`
- French `fr`
- German `de`
- Italian `it`
- Portuguese `pt`
- Russian `ru`
- Indonesian `id`
- Chinese `zh`
- Dutch `nl`
- Polish `pl`
- Swedish `sv`

<Note>
  The model might work on other launguages we haven't tested. Feel free to try
  it on launguages that are not listed above and provide us with feedback.
</Note>
