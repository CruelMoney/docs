Moderation API is a full stack content moderation solution that helps you streamline your content moderation. You get state-of-the-art AI models for automated content moderation, a full-featured moderation dashboard for manual moderation, analytics for your content, and cutomizable workflows to integrate with your platform.

Moderation API allows you to easily:

- Automate content moderation
- Create custom moderation workflows
- Scale your platform safely
- Identify bad actors
- Get insights into your content
- Build self-learning AI models
- Hire human moderators
